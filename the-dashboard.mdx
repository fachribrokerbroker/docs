The **Dashboard** is your home screen. It's a cockpit, not a landing page: it pulls the things that need you to the top so you can act without hunting through tabs.

## What you'll see

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Greeting & stat tiles">
    A quick line-up of your live numbers — **active listings**, **live buyers**, **open opportunities**, and **deals in flight**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Opportunities hero">
    Your strongest current matches — buyers of yours that fit other brokers' live listings. Jump straight in to express interest.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Deals waiting on you">
    Deals where the next move is yours — an interest to accept, a completion to confirm, a price to enter.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Pipeline funnel">
    Where your deals sit across the lifecycle, from interested through to completed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listings needing attention">
    Properties that could use a nudge — missing a fee, unlaunched, or going quiet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Trust & health">
    Your Buddy Score / verification status, and an account-health banner if anything needs fixing (for example, an incomplete card).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Treat the dashboard as a to-do list. If a card is showing, it's usually because something there is waiting on you.
</Tip>
