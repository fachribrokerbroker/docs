---

<ResponseField name="Property / Listing">
  Something you're selling. It only starts matching once **launched**.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Buyer">
  A saved search for one of your clients — budget, location, type, and beds. Matching runs continuously against it.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Opportunity">
  A match: one of your buyers fits another broker's live listing. Shown with a match score and the offered fee.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Match score">
  How well a listing fits a buyer's brief. **Price** and **location** must both fit; **type**, **beds**, **size**, and **amenities** are scored when you've specified them. A listing only surfaces if it passes the mandatory checks, so scores you see are already qualified matches.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Deal">
  A co-broking thread between the **referring broker** (brought the buyer) and the **listing broker** (owns the property), from interest through completion.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Sub-agent fee">
  The fee the listing broker pays the referring broker for introducing a buyer — a percentage of the sale price, set on the listing and **locked when both brokers accept**.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Circle">
  A private group of brokers used to share **pocket** (off-market) listings.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Pocket vs public">
  **Public** listings are visible to the whole network. **Pocket** listings are visible only to the circles you share them with.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Vouch">
  A cross-firm endorsement from another broker. Only one per firm counts toward standing.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Buddy Score">
  A reputation signal from your deal track record and ratings — how reliably you close and how counterparties rate you.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Verification (blue tick)">
  A credential check. Submit a licence under **Get verified**; once approved you earn the blue tick. Separate from your Buddy Score.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Market">
  The region you operate in (for example, UK or Dubai). Set during onboarding — it drives currency, matching, and which features apply.
</ResponseField>
