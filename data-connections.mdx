**Data connections** let you bring listings in from an external CRM instead of adding them by hand. Today that's **Loop** (available in supported markets, currently the UK).

Find it under **Settings → Data connections**.

## Connect Loop

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Data connections">
    If Loop is available for your market, you'll see it here. If you don't see it, your market isn't eligible yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Enter your Loop details and connect. Use **Test connection** first if you want to confirm the credentials before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync">
    Your Loop listings sync into BrokerBroker. Use **Sync now** to pull the latest at any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After syncing

Imported properties appear under [Properties](/properties) like any other listing. Review them, set your **sub-agent fee**, and **launch** the ones you want on the network — importing doesn't launch them automatically.

## Disconnect

Use **Disconnect** on the same page to stop syncing. Already-imported listings stay in your account.
