The **notification bell** keeps you current on everything that needs you — new matches for your buyers, deal updates (accepted, in progress, completed), circle invites, and network activity.

## The feed

Open the bell to see your feed, grouped by **Today / Yesterday / This week / Earlier**. Unread items are highlighted. Tap any notification to jump straight to the thing it's about — the deal, the opportunity, or the listing.

- **Mark all as read** clears the unread count.

## Preferences

Under notification **preferences** you control which categories you're notified about. The in-app feed and real-time updates are always on; preferences fine-tune the rest.

<Tip>
  Match and deal notifications are the ones you don't want to miss — a match notification is often the first sign of a deal. Tapping it takes you to the opportunity or deal to act.
</Tip>
