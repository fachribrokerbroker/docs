Open **Settings** from the sidebar. It has a few sections:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Account">
    Manage your profile details, change your email (confirmed by a verification email), and manage your password. This is also where you can **delete your account** — see below.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Appearance">
    Switch the portal between light and dark, and adjust display preferences.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Data connections">
    Connect and sync an external CRM. See [Data connections](/data-connections).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Deleting your account

Account deletion is permanent and cascades across the platform:

- Your **public card is taken down and de-indexed** (removed from search).
- **Vouches you gave** are invalidated, and affected brokers' standing recomputes.
- Records tied to completed deals are preserved but anonymised.

<Warning>
  Deletion can't be undone. If you only want to step back temporarily, unpublish your card and pause your buyers instead.
</Warning>
