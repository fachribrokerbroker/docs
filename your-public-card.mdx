Your **card** is your public profile on BrokerBroker — a page buyers and other brokers can view and share, built to be found by search engines and AI assistants.

## Edit your card

Open **My card**. You can set:

- **Headline & bio** — write your own, or use **Auto-generate** for a first draft, then edit.
- **Cover & photo** — upload your own cover image or pick a ready-made one.
- **Specialisms, languages, and operating areas** — what you do and where.
- **Social links** — LinkedIn, Instagram.

Changes save to your card. A **completion** meter shows what's left to add.

## Publish

Click **Publish** to make your card live. Publishing is open — you don't need to be verified first; an unverified card simply renders as unverified.

Once published, the status panel shows your **public card URL** (for example, `brokerbroker.app/uk/your-name`). Use **Copy link** or **Open public card** to view and share it.

## Get verified

Submit a licence or credential under **Get verified**. After an admin reviews it, your card earns the **blue tick**. See [Vouches & trust](/vouches) for how verification differs from your Buddy Score.

## What buyers see

The public card shows your headline, bio, specialisms, areas, verification state, vouches you've chosen to display, and your track record. It's designed to be **crawlable and shareable**, so it works as a link you can drop anywhere.
