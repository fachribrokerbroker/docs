BrokerBroker connects verified agents, buyers, and listings — matched automatically, around the clock. You register what you have and who you're looking for; the platform surfaces the overlaps and gives you a safe, fee-locked way to close deals with other brokers.

This guide covers the **broker portal** at [my.brokerbroker.app](https://my.brokerbroker.app).
