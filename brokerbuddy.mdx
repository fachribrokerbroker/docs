**BrokerBuddy** is your assistant inside BrokerBroker. Open it from the sidebar to chat: ask how something works, get a hand drafting a listing or a buyer search, or check on your pipeline.

## What it can help with

- **Answer questions** about using the platform.
- **Draft listings** — describe a property in plain language and it can turn that into a launch-ready draft.
- **Capture buyers** — describe what a client wants and it can set up the buyer search.
- **Summarise** what's happening across your properties, buyers, and deals.

## One conversation, everywhere

BrokerBuddy is the same assistant across the **web portal, the mobile app, and WhatsApp**, and it shares one history — so a conversation you start on WhatsApp continues in the portal, and vice versa.

<Tip>
  Anything BrokerBuddy drafts is yours to review before it goes live. Confirm listings and buyer searches the same way you would if you'd typed them in.
</Tip>
