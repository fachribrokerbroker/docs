**Stats** brings together two lenses on your performance.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Engagement">
    How your [public card](/your-public-card) is doing — views and interactions over time. Views are de-duplicated so repeat visits from the same person in a day don't inflate the numbers, giving you an honest reach figure (including how discoverable your card is to AI assistants and crawlers).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Performance">
    Your deal activity — what's in flight, what you've closed, and how you compare over time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Low engagement usually means an incomplete or unpublished card. If your numbers are flat, start with [Your public card](/your-public-card).
</Tip>
