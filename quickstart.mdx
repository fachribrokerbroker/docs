This walkthrough gets you fully set up. Budget about ten minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [my.brokerbroker.app](https://my.brokerbroker.app) and sign up with Google, Apple, or email. See [Signing in & onboarding](/signing-in) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish onboarding">
    Answer three quick questions — your **market**, the **cities/areas** you work, and your **specialisms**. This tunes matching to you. You can't be matched correctly until your market is set, so don't skip it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish your card">
    Head to **My card**, add a headline, bio, and photo, then **Publish**. Your public profile is now live and shareable. See [Your public card](/your-public-card).

    <Tip>
      An unverified card is fine to publish — submit a credential later under **Get verified** to earn the blue tick.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add and launch a property">
    Open **Properties → New property**, fill in the details, set your **sub-agent fee**, choose **who can see it**, and **Launch**. See [Properties](/properties).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register a buyer">
    Open **Buyers → New buyer** and capture the brief — budget, location, type, beds. Matching starts immediately. See [Buyers](/buyers).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work your first opportunity">
    When a buyer matches someone else's listing, it appears under **Opportunities**. Open it, review the match, and **Express interest** to open a deal. See [Opportunities](/opportunities) and [Deals](/deals).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where things live

The sidebar is grouped into three areas:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Workspace">
    **Dashboard**, **Properties**, **Buyers**, **Opportunities**, **Deals** — the day-to-day pipeline.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Network & trust">
    **Circles**, **Vouches**, **My card**, **Stats** — your reputation and reach.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Tools">
    **BrokerBuddy** (your assistant) and **Help**. Account, appearance, and data connections live under **Settings**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
